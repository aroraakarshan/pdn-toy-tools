What is EMIR?

EMIR (Electromigration and IR Drop) is a critical challenge in modern VLSI design. Electromigration is the gradual displacement of metal atoms in a semiconductor, which can cause interconnects to fail. IR Drop is the voltage drop in the power delivery network, which can lead to timing violations and reduced performance. These tools provide an intuitive, visual way to understand and analyze these complex phenomena.
